The official OSK Academy Black Belt Grading examination, set and examined by Renshi Sajesh P. — 5th Dan Black Belt, WKF Coach. This page reproduces the syllabus in its own order and illustrates each domain with reference video, so the whole road to black belt can be revised in one place.

進身後退 — Shinshin Kotai

PT-2 — Shinshin Kotai (17 combinations)

"Advancing and retreating." The basics of PT-1 are chained into seventeen combinations, performed stepping forward and back, both sides. Note: the syllabus titles this Kotai (後退, retreating) — not "gōtai" — the drill is about moving in and out, not a "body-spirit" phrase.

一本組手 — Ippon Kumite

PT-3 — Ippon Kumite (10, incl. locks & throws)

One-step pre-arranged sparring with a partner. The attacker commits a single named attack; the candidate receives and counters with the prescribed response — ending, in the final two, with joint locks and throws.

組手 — Kumite

Kumite — the grading rounds

Free fighting under fatigue, scaling to multiple opponents. The full kumite ladder, ma-ai, vital points and WKF scoring are on the Kumite page.

One vs. multiple opponents — kumite training reference

  1. One opponent — 5 rounds. Consecutive jiyū-kumite against a fresh partner each round.
  2. Two opponents — 3 rounds. Two attackers at once; manage angles so they cannot both reach you.
  3. Three opponents — 2 rounds. Keep moving, never let all three line up on you.
  4. Street fighting. Practical self-defense — grabs, holds, no-rules pressure. Control and decisiveness over sport scoring.

試し割り — Tameshiwari

Tiles breaking — Tameshiwari

A test of focused power (kime) and, as much, of spirit. Breaking is technique with no margin for hesitation — the strike either commits fully or it fails. Train under supervision only.

指導 — Shidō

Beyond technique — the black belt's duties

Shodan is the point at which the student begins to give back. Three demonstrations test whether the candidate can teach, create, and officiate — not just perform.

指導 · Shidō

Conduct a class

Lead a section of training for half an hour — warm-up, basics, a kata, correction. Tests command, clarity, and reigi as an instructor.

創作 · Sōsaku

Own combinations

Create and demonstrate original combination forms and their application — proving the syllabus has been internalised, not just memorised.

審判 · Shinpan

Refereeing & judging

Officiate kumite — calls, scoring, and penalties. The WKF scoring reference is on the Kumite page.

Two months before the test

Confirm everything on this page with Renshi Sajesh or Kyōshi Chandran — exact technique names, move counts, which kata and bunkai, how many kumite rounds, the breaking requirement, and the endurance gate. This site is preparation; Sensei is the syllabus.
